Disruption is our mission

Our ultimate purpose at CHAOS is to create better and more sustainable cities for everyone around the world.
The success of a startup consists of two things: the right moment in the market and a bold team to execute the idea.

Our multicultural team is composed by exceptional talent leading in the tech and smart city industry. Business women and men, change-drivers, nerds, speakers, thinkers and doers. We value team work, progressive thinking, passion and grit.

Our core values

Purpose
Giving back to society is important for us. We aim to offer solutions to promote sustainable city growth and the social well-being of its citizens. We work towards creating better cities – cities that are meant to last in time by being capable of adapting to the future needs of citizens or, in other words, cities that are sustainable and liveable.
Expertise

A bold innovation and an ambitious vision require a fearless executing team. Our biggest asset is our passion for what we do. People are our motivation to pursue innovation, as we strongly believe that this world should be a better place for everyone. We have a strong PhD background, specialised in urban planning, real estate, and data science.

Humbleness

We encourage openness, trust, and recognition, and make sure we surround ourselves with bright and inspiring minds from the ecosystem. We foster a culture of development where others’ needs, strengths and contributions are key. This applies to how we work with our customers, partners, and between each other.
