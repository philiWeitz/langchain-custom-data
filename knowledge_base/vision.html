CHAOS is the urban forecasting company that creates liveable cities

After facing one of the greatest pandemics of our time, we can now argue that decisions based on outdated data and personal knowledge are not sustainable. We need to look forward, detect trends, and understand how the world and its citizens are changing, especially in city development.

Making cities sustainable means creating safe and affordable housing and building resilient societies and economies. It involves investment in public infrastructure, green public spaces, and improving urban planning and management in participatory and inclusive ways. This can only be achieved by taking decisions that are backed by fact-based insights and accurate long-term forecasts.

Sustainable urban development
powered by technology, data, and people.

Liveable cities

Our ultimate goal is to provide our customers with a comprehensive view of urban environments to develop services, urban infrastructure, and residential and commercial spaces so that they will benefit the society, bring profitability, and ensure the liveability and sustainability of our cities.

People at the core

We believe in the alliance of human intelligence and innovation. Putting residents and tenants at the heart of your plans is essential to successful real estate development.

Forecast the future needs of your city, and evaluate the present by understanding socio-economic fabrics, and demographic trends.

Data-driven decisions

Embracing the power of data is key for running sustainable and high-value urban projects.

Through data and technology, we transform traditional market analysis into efficient, fact-based, and on-demand insights that enable better decision-making in real estate investment and development.

We work to create a more liveable world for everyone
